Sankalp – "A Cosmic Resolution"

We’re thrilled to introduce TEDxSRMIST Trichy—a student-led, independently organized TEDx event. Rooted in the ancient wisdom of intention and aligned with the boundless potential of the cosmos, Sankalp reflects a commitment that reaches beyond the self. Through compelling talks by students, faculty, and visionary minds, we aim to spark not just ideas—but purposeful resolutions that resonate on a universal scale. This is more than an event—it’s a call to align inner purpose with collective progress. Together, we pledge to push boundaries, drive meaningful change, and shape a future guided by intention, innovation, and cosmic responsibility.
