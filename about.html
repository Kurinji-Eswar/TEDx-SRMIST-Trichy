TED, founded in 1984, is a global community dedicated to sharing "ideas worth spreading." Owned by a nonprofit foundation, TED aims to make great ideas accessible and spark meaningful conversations. Through its TEDx program, local communities and organizations can host TED-like events to foster connection and dialogue.

TEDx is a grassroots initiative created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events.

Vision

To cultivate a dynamic platform at SRM Institute of Science and Technology where ideas ignite change, voices inspire progress, and diverse perspectives shape a better future—empowering individuals to think deeply, act boldly, and innovate responsibly.

Mission

Our mission is to amplify powerful ideas by organizing thought-provoking TED-style events that bring together students, faculty, alumni, and changemakers from all walks of life. We aim to foster a culture of intellectual curiosity, creativity, and social responsibility by:

  • Showcasing transformative stories and groundbreaking research
  • Encouraging interdisciplinary dialogue and critical thinking
  • Inspiring the SRM community to drive positive change locally and globally

Through these efforts, we seek to contribute meaningfully to the global TEDx movement while reflecting the academic excellence and innovation ethos of SRM Institute of Science and Technology.
